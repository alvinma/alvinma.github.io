Japan - September 26th to October 10th, 2019

The amazing Japan highlights!

It has been a while since I have gone on an oversea trip, and this trip was a huge success in my endevors as it had opened up my mind and made me want to travel to world a whole lot more. The culture, food, cleanliness, infrastructure; everything was astouding!! My experience in Japan makes me want to see what the rest of the world has to offer. Let me not bore you with any more details. Let me show you what I was able to capture during my experience in Japan...

Insert a collage of awesome experiences of Japan here.

On My Way to Japan!!

It has been a while since I had flown internationally, but this experience brought a wonderful experience! The greatest lesson I picked up was to travel with no, or close to none, expectations!! My flight was with United Airlines and my expectations on this flight was extremely low as I only heard about not so great things about flying with United Airlines, but even though I had the middle seat of a three seat row, it wasn't too bad. The mother and baby on my left was pretty nice and interesting to watch, the guy, Matthew, to my right was travelling to Tokyo only for the four day weekend for his girlfriend (True commitment right there!!), and I had a lot of movies and anime to watch!

The food wasn't bad either! One of my concerns was that the food would be unbarable, but because of my low expectations, the food ended up being pretty edible. I was also served a lemon serbert for desert! It was too sweet and I was not able to finish it, but the effort was seriously appreciated. Overall, it was a descent flight. And on the plus side, Alladin live action movie was pretty entertaining to watch!

Insert plane photo here.

As soon as I landed in Tokyo, my adventure begins and I spent the rest of the day figuring out the infrastructure of Japan. The subway system was just simply impressive, but once you consistently take the train, you'll be able to master it in two days as there is literally only one line that you'll take over and over again. The Yamamoto Line for the WIN!

To relinquish a crave I had since I last gone to Japan in 2007, I directly went to Ichiran Ramen, lined up for thirty minutes, and ordered the largest order they had! Yes! My first meal was Ichiran Ramen and it was delicious!!

Sad Realization of Tokyo...

On my first day at Toyko, I was excited to go out and roam the city in expectation that the city was similar in size to a nearby home city, San Francisco... After my first day of exploration, I was surprised to find out that Tokyo was nothing like San Francisco. Toyko was ENORMOUS compared to San Francisco! After some research, San Francisco is roughly 47 square miles (121.73 square kilometers), whereas Tokyo is roughly 845 square miles (2188.55 square kilometers)!! This was just estonishing! I was not able cover a piece of Tokyo in the day, and there was just so many activities happening everyday!
