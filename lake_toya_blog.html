The Lake View Toya Nonokaze Resort

Going to this resort would probably be the highlight of my Japan trip! It was the sole reason why I decided to develop this website and continue my travelling adventures! Having a lake view from my hotel room and my first japanese outdoor hot spring experience as well seriously made this stay a treat!

From the customer services, to the lobby, to the room, to the buffet, and finally the hot spring, everything was just awesome! Immediately walking into the resort, we were pleasantly greeted to the lobby and then served a pleasant fruit drink with a cracker snack. When checking in, the Standard Twin Room that I booked was upgraded to the Deluxe Room with a Tatami Area. The upgraded room made the experience a whole lot better as it was a lot roomier with a mixture of both western and japanese style.

#2. Creative WordPress Themes

In summary, ~$200 per person may be expensive, but the experience was amazing!! It's a place where I never wanted to leave!
