Simple Cubed Chicken Breast Receipe

BLOG STILL IN PROGRESS!!!

The Reason..

The reason for the development of this recipe was for my fitness goals. I had gone through many many experiments to get to this point. For my fitness goals, I need to intake a lot of protein, so I have been experimenting multiple methods of cooking chicken breast. Chicken breast is one of the leanest and healthiest methods of attaining a good amount of protein. The nutrition facts can be found here, where 43 grams of protein is served per cup of chicken breast. Even though it is extremely simple, the following recipe is what I found to have the best outcome.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Egg
  • Corn Starch
  • Vegetable/Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Preperation:

  1. Cut the chicken breast into cubes for quicker cooking and put it on a plate or bowl.
  2. Beat the egg and mix the salt and pepper seasoning with the egg.
  3. Pour the beated and seasoned egg with the cubed chicken breast and mix well.
  4. Evenly layer the top of the chicken breast with corn starch as if you are frying the chicken breast. This is to retain the moister of the chicken!
  5. Put the plate or bowl into the freeze for approximately 20-30 minutes!
Total Time for Preperation: 30-40 minutes

Direction:

  1. Heat the pan and pour some oil onto the pan. Ensure that the oil and pan are heated well!
  2. Set the temperature down to medium and drop the prepared cubed chicken breast into the pan
  3. Evenly spread the cubed chicken breast and ensure that no chicken pieces are stacked on top of each other.
  4. Close the lid and cook for 1 minute.
  5. After 1 minute, flip all the chicken pieces over. This should take 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  6. Close the lid and cook for another 1 minute.
  7. Once the last 3 steps are completed, while keeping the lid on, take the pan off the heat and set it aside. We will let the chicken slow cook. This will take roughly 3-5 minutes.
  8. Pour the chicken on a bowl with a layer of napkins on the bottom to absorb the excess oil.
  9. Remove napkin layer and serve!
Total Time to Cook: 6-8 minutes

Side Notes:

To add some side notes to this recipe, please experiment the heat leveling at your own pace. While experimenting this recipe, I have found that the heat exertion differed between the stove top I have been using. In general, this recipe will cook the chicken thorougly, but it may end up on the drier side. Therefore, heat experimentation may be required.

Paragraph

This is my simple and efficient method of cooking chicken breast for my nightly meals for protein intake!
